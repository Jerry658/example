Distribution Question 2 (2 marks)

Distribution Question 3 (2 marks)

3. Each die in the game is a standard six-sided die. Define the probability mass function for the distribution of a single die roll. What is the expected value and variance of a single die roll? Additionally, calculate the covariance between the red and green dice rolls.

Distribution Question 4 (3 marks)

Distribution Question 5 (3 marks)
